Authentic Mexican dishes for the whole family to enjoy!

Baby Salsa is your destination for authentic Mexican food. Our menu includes all your favourite dishes, like tacos, burritos, quesadillas, flautas, and more! We are also proud to offer a large selection of vegan options for many of our dishes. For example, our Vegan Sin Carne Quesadilla comes with black beans, corn, bell pepper, tomato, green onion, jalapeno, and Daiya cheese board shreds, served with a side of rice, while our Vegan Flautas Ahogadas is a delicious crispy corn tortilla taquitos filled with vegan chorizo smothered in your choice of ranchera salsa or salsa verde, and is served with rice, beans, and guacamole on the side.

With two locations in Nanaimo including our Express location, as well as online ordering, pickup, and delivery through SkipTheDishes, there are plenty of options for you to get your next fix of delicious Mexican cuisine!
